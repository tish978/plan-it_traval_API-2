About

My Story

Hi! My name is Satish Bisa. I've always wanted to travel, but... I've never known where to start!

Where do I go? How can I be confident I'm spending my money wisely? These are all important, yet daunting questions I struggle to answer. And sometimes it has held me back from traveling. I never understood why travel planning had to be so hard sometimes!

But what if travel planning was made easy?

What if you could just fill out a form, and then be recommended the best personalized destinations across the globe for YOU to go to? What if all I had to do was just go and make memories?

This was what inspired be to create Plan-It Travel. A one-stop location for ANYBODY to make their dreams of traveling come true.
